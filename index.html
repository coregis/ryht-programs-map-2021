The Raise Your Hand Texas Programs Map is a data visualization platform developed by Core GIS.
Get Started With the Raise Your Hand Texas Programs Map: Map: Visualize the growth of Raise Your Hand Texas Programs (Raising Blended Learners, Raising School Leaders, and Raising Texas Teachers: University Partners and Charles Butt Scholars) from 2008 to the present. Interaction: In the sidebar on the left hand side, click the Play, Stop, Skip Forward, or Skip Backward buttons to explore the reach of our programs across the state of Texas. You can toggle viewing or hiding each Program, and view by House or Senate District. Finally, you can click on each dot to learn more about a particular program, partnership, or recipient.
